Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I order?

We recommend placing your order at least 2 weeks in advance for custom cakes, and 1-2 months for wedding cakes.

Do you offer tastings?

Yes, we offer complimentary tastings for wedding cakes. For other custom cakes, tasting sessions can be arranged for a small fee.

What is your delivery area?

We deliver throughout Nairobi and surrounding areas. Delivery fees vary based on location.

What is your cancellation policy?

Orders can be cancelled up to 1 week before the event date for a full refund. Custom orders require a 50% deposit.
